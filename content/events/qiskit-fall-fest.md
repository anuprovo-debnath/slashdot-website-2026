The second edition of the IBM Qiskit Fall Fest at IISER Kolkata, celebrating 100 years of quantum mechanics. This multi-day event brings together students and experts to explore quantum technologies through hands-on workshops and specialized lectures.

**Event Schedule Highlights:**
* **Main Fest (Oct 24-26):** Workshops on qubits, gates, entanglement, and error correction.
* **Grand Finale (Oct 30):** Expert talk and Panel Discussion from 17:30 - 19:30 IST.
* **Finale Venue:** Ashima Chatterjee Lecture Hall Theatre, TRC 2nd Floor.
